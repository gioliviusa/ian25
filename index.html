Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about IAN

What is IAN Finance?

IAN Finance is a decentralized finance (DeFi) token built on Binance Smart Chain that offers innovative financial solutions including reflection rewards, auto-liquidity generation, and a growing ecosystem of products designed to empower users in the DeFi space.

How do I buy IAN tokens?

You can purchase IAN on PancakeSwap by swapping BNB for IAN. First, ensure you have BNB in your wallet (Trust Wallet or MetaMask), connect to PancakeSwap, and use our official contract address: 0x07d5bf841beb877c9bE550B79cb0d49e4e091Dec

What are the transaction taxes?

IAN has a 3% total transaction tax: 1% distributed to holders as reflection rewards, 1% added to liquidity, and 1% permanently burned. These mechanisms create passive income, price stability, and deflationary pressure.

Is IAN audited?

Yes, the IAN smart contract has been thoroughly audited by reputable blockchain security firms to ensure safety and reliability. Audit reports are available on our website for full transparency.

Where can I learn more?

Join our Telegram community for real-time updates, read our detailed whitepaper, or follow us on Twitter for announcements. Our team is always available to answer questions from community members.
