FAQ

What is IAN Finance?

IAN Finance is a decentralized finance (DeFi) token built on Binance Smart Chain that offers innovative financial solutions including reflection rewards, auto-liquidity generation, and a growing ecosystem of products designed to empower users in the DeFi space.

How do I buy IAN tokens?

You can purchase IAN on PancakeSwap by swapping BNB for IAN. Use our official contract address: 0x07d5bf841beb877c9bE550B79cb0d49e4e091Dec

What are the transaction taxes?

IAN has a 3% total transaction tax: 1% distributed to holders as reflection rewards, 1% added to liquidity, and 1% permanently burned.

Is IAN audited?

Yes, the IAN smart contract has been thoroughly audited by reputable blockchain security firms. Audit reports are available on our website.
